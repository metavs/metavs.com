Frequently Asked Questions

Last Modified Jan 2, 2021
What makes a monster unique?
Monsters will be assigned randomized base stats and a 'grade' ( Common, Rare, Super Rare, Ultra Rare, Secret Rare ) during the hatching process.
Grades represent a monsters 'potential' and each grade modifies the monsters color palette giving it a unique aesthetic in our universe and marketplace.
When a monster reaches a new level it will receive 1 stat point.
Stat points: Can only be modified by the current owner. Cannot be shared or transferred to other monsters. Once a stat point is used the action cannot be revoked.
Stats do not have to be set at the time of leveling. Trainers can choose to sell monsters with unused stat points or assign stats when convenient.
As trainers customize their monster builds this will lead to unique monsters with different grades and stat branches.
What are the benefits of owning Generation 0 Metamon?

By owning Generation 0 monsters you will become a pillar of the MetaVS community. You will receive breeding perks designed to grow the population of each species to sell or trade with the rest of the community.

Once we reach 1 Million players Generation 0 owners will have to breed a minimum of 100 offspring to supply each player with 1 monster. Offspring can be sold individually, in a mystery egg raffle, giveaway, etc.

This model can be repeated for every monster species. Players will need duplicates of the same species to create different stat builds, train monsters with better grades, etc. We designed the breeding model around Generation 0 monsters to create a 'breeder' role within the community. Breeders will become an important backbone of the MetaVS.

How many MetaPacks will be available?

Each pack may have varying quantities but we will likely mint 10,000 packs per drop. This will provide equal pairs of each Generation 0 monster in the pack ( 5k female, 5k male ).

Our team will reserve 2k packs to populate the game world with wild catchable Generation 1+ monsters.

Which marketplace will MetaPacks be listed on?
