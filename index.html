Roadmap

MetaVS is a 2.5D sandbox and Web 3.0 platform built in the browser. The game will be available across all devices with a web browser. We aim to create the easiest, no-code solution to NFT based sandbox games.
Chapter I
Launch MetaPass, create the MetaVS token, and release alpha world editor with exploration.
Chapter II

Chapter III

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MetaVS?

MetaVS or Metaverse is creating a fully explorable game where operators capture monsters to battle, train, breed, and use to play & earn. We have developed an easy-to-use editor that allows people to drag and drop nft (assets) on user lands or private worlds for standalone use of our engine.

Why MetaVS is special?

METAVS has created our Metaverse from scratch. We offer a user-friendly interface for creators accessible on all devices. We are also able to create a multi-chain system to interact with NFTs from all chains (ethereum, solana etc). Our Platform will be open to public creation of Monsters/Avatars/Lands for Player Houses/ Private Worlds (for clubs, nfts, social events) /Tournaments, Ladders & Wagers
